Lollapalooza 2021

Lollapalooza officially started in 1991 as a touring musical festival that toured North America, but after a six year break (1977-2003) Lollapalooza came back to life and has been taking place in Chicago for over 20 years. Lollapalooza has now been expanded globally, there are locations in Berlin, SÃ£o Paulo, Buenos Aires, Santiago, Stockholm, Paris, and Mumbai. In 2021 Lollapalooza was very special and sentimental, it marked its 30th year aniversary and it's first year returning after the Covid 19 pandemic. In response to its return, the festival was sold out and at its capacity with is 385,000 attendees over the span of four days. The lineup for Lollapalooza for 2021 was very diverse and unique. There were a mix of hip hop artists such as Megan Thee Stallion and Tyler the Creator, country artist such as Miley Cirus, r&b artists such as Giveon, and pop artists such as Kim Petras.
