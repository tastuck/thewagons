Dreamville Festival 2025

Dreamville Festival started in 2019 and was created by J.Cole; his reasons for starting Dreamville fest are: celebrating Dreamville artists and fans, giving back to his hometown/community, and creating a family-friendly environment for all. Dreamville fest has been operating for five years (2019, 2021-2025) and 2025 is its last year in operation. In 2025, there was a lot of diversity from the artists performing to the fans in the audience. Dreamville fest took place on April 5-6 and there were many artists in the lineup such as 21 Savage, Ludacris, Keyshia Cole, Chief Keef, Wale, Glorilla, Erykah Badu, and many more talented artist. For this music festival there were over 104,000 attendees from all 50 states and from over 20 countries.
