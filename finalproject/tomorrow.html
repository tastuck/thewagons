Tomorrowland 2024

Tomorrowland is an EDM festival hosted annually in Boom, Belgium. Created in 2004, Tomorrowland has become one of the most successful music festivals in the world. What started as a single day festival is now a two weekend event every July. Since it's creation, two "spin-off" festivals have been created as well, in Brazil and France. It is known for its vast and elaborate stages. Some are permanent, while others change based off that years theme.
