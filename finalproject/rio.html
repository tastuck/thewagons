Rock in Rio 2017

Rock in Rio is a music festival held in Rio de Janeiro biennially. The event normally last 6-10 days. Despite its name, Rock in Rio has taken on several variations including events hosted in Portugal, Spain, and the US. The first iteration hosted in 1985 brought in around 1.38 million people. Following the 2016 Olympic Summer Games, Rock in Rio began using Barra Olympic Park as its new venue, and it remains the case to this day.
